Our Services

Quotes

Dynasty 8 is able to quote any property throughout the city, however we will quote the property based upon our price sheet not sentimental value.

Financing

Dynasty 8 finances up to $900,000! Simply place 30% down, then $40,000 minimum payments are due every 14 days!

Selling

Dynasty 8 sells brand-new houses all across San Andreas from Los Santos to Blaine County. In order to sell a house it must be recorded and unowned.

Smartlocks

Dynasty 8 sells smartlocks which are accessible through your phone app and allow you to add people by their CID, as well as to lock and unlock your house remotely from your phone. The price is $10,000.

Garage Slots

Dynasty 8 provides garage slots for all properties. All properties come with 3 garage slots however you can upgrade any property to 10 garage slots, and each garage slot costs $10,000.

Upgrades

Currently, you are able to upgrade your warehouse from whatever size it is to the next available size and only pay the price difference. The building must fit the warehouse for it to be eligible for the upgrade.
