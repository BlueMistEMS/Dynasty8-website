What is the per-person property limit?

Dynasty 8 currently enforces a two property limit for all persons. Exceptions may be made in certain circumstances, such as:
- 1. If Dynasty 8 is presented with a presentation/some type of plan for the properties other than simply for personal use.
- 2. Individuals who own businesses are sometimes granted an additional property for their business when deemed necessary by Dynasty 8.

Does Dynasty 8 offer financing?

Yes, Dynasty 8 finances up to $900,000! Simply place 30% down, then $40,000 minimum payments are due every 14 days!

Does Dynasty 8 buy back properties?

Currently, Dynasty 8 does not have a buy-back program, however, it is something we are looking into for the future.

What can be a warehouse door?

When deciding if a door can be a warehouse or not, we look for a few different things:
- The building should match the door, for example industrial areas with industrial warehouse doors will be able to be warehouses.
- The door should not be on the same building as a storefront location, as there is a possibility of that being a business in the future.

Is Dynasty 8 hiring?

Dynasty 8 will always advertise when we're hiring.

Dynasty 8 will always advertise in the #rp-advertisements email channel as well as in Yellow Pages when we are hiring. If you do not see advertisements there, you can check this link to see if applications are open at this time.

Do you have a list of available properties?

Currently, Dynasty 8 does not keep track of all available houses.

The best way to check to see if something is available is to visit the house, walk up to the door and look for a blue or green doorbell. If there is no blue and green doorbell, it likely means the property is available for purchase.
